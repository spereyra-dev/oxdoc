# Code of Conduct

`oxdoc` aims to be a useful, low-drama technical project. Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep discussions respectful, focused, and actionable.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being dismissive.
- Assume good intent, but clarify concrete problems.
- Keep technical criticism about the work, not the person.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Accept maintainer decisions once a direction is settled.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests.
- Spam or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete comments; close issues or PRs; or block users when behavior harms the project. Serious reports should be sent privately to the maintainers instead of opened as public issues.
